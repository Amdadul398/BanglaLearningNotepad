Common Bangla Letters & Sounds

অ

Pronunciation: "o" as in "hot"

Example: অনেক (onek) - many

আ

Pronunciation: "a" as in "father"

Example: আম (am) - mango

ই

Pronunciation: "i" as in "bit"

Example: ইচ্ছা (iccha) - wish

ঈ

Pronunciation: "ee" as in "feet"

Example: ঈদ (eed) - Eid

উ

Pronunciation: "u" as in "put"

Example: উৎসব (utsob) - festival

ঊ

Pronunciation: "oo" as in "food"

Example: ঊষা (usha) - dawn
